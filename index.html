ABOUT ROLLING DICE APPAREL

Founded in 2025, Rolling Dice Apparel emerged from a vision to revolutionize urban fashion. We believe that style is a gamble worth taking, and every piece in our collection represents a bold statement of individuality and confidence.

Our brand philosophy centers around the idea that fashion is about taking risks and expressing your authentic self. From premium tracksuits to statement hoodies, each item is carefully crafted to embody the spirit of those who dare to be different.

Meet Our Founders

Paballo Motjela

Co-Founder & Creative Director

Paballo brings a unique vision to urban fashion with over 8 years of experience in design and brand development. Growing up in Johannesburg, he developed a passion for street culture and fashion that speaks to the youth. His creative genius lies in blending contemporary streetwear with premium quality materials, creating pieces that are both stylish and durable. Paballo's design philosophy centers around empowering individuals to express their authentic selves through bold fashion choices.

Chef Otis

Co-Founder & Operations Manager

Chef Otis is the operational backbone of Rolling Dice Apparel, bringing 6 years of business management and logistics expertise to the brand. Known for his meticulous attention to detail and customer-first approach, he ensures that every aspect of the business runs smoothly from supply chain management to customer service. His background in hospitality has instilled in him the importance of exceptional service, which he has seamlessly integrated into the fashion retail experience. Chef's strategic thinking and operational excellence have been instrumental in establishing Rolling Dice Apparel as a trusted premium brand.
